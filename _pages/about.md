Jiale is currently  a Ph.D. candidate in [the Department of Computer Science, Shanghai Jiao Tong University](https://cs.sjtu.edu.cn/cse/en/), supervised by Prof. [Yanyan Shen](https://shen-yy.github.io/).  He is also an intern at ByteDance, advised by Dr. [Xiaogang Shi](https://shixiaogang.com/). Previously, he received his B.S. from [the College of Computer Science, Sichuan University](https://cs.scu.edu.cn/e_jsjxy/).

His previous work mainly focuses on explaining graph neural networks, and he is currently working on data selection and data debugging for machine learning models, especially for large language models.

# Research Interests

Data Selection; Data Debugging; Retrieval-Augmented Generation; Trustworthy AI

He has organized his recent reading paper list in [awesome-ml-data-quality-papers](https://github.com/SJTU-DMTai/awesome-ml-data-quality-papers).
